Features

Designed with South African Women in Mind

Our platform offers comprehensive tools for both patients and doctors to manage women's health needs.

Appointment Scheduling

Book, reschedule or cancel appointments with verified female gynecologists at your convenience.

Digital Medical Records

Securely store and access your medical history, test results, and prescriptions in one place.

E-Prescriptions

Receive digital prescriptions that can be sent directly to your preferred pharmacy.

Secure Messaging

Private and encrypted communication with your healthcare provider when you need advice.

Process

How Safe for Women Works

1

Register Your Account

Patients and doctors create accounts with verified South African ID numbers (only female users accepted).

2

Complete Your Profile

Patients add health details. Doctors provide practice information and qualifications for verification.

3

Connect & Manage

Patients can book appointments, view records. Doctors manage practice, patients, and prescriptions.
